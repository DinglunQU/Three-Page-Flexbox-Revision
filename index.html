The absence of a centralized hub for car modification enthusiasts worldwide has long been a gaping void in the automotive community. Despite the countless passionate individuals tinkering with engines, customizing exteriors, and fine-tuning every aspect of their vehicles, there hasn't been a unified platform to connect and celebrate their shared passion. This lack of organization not only disperses valuable knowledge but also deprives enthusiasts of the camaraderie and support that comes from a dedicated community. Recognizing this critical gap, a group of visionary individuals came together in the heart of New York City in 2024 to remedy this issue once and for all.
